🔄 Symmetry & Transformations

🪞 Reflection Flip over a line (mirror image)
🔃 Rotation Turn around a point (90°, 180°, 270°)
➡️ Translation Slide in any direction (shape stays the same)
All 3 transformations keep the shape the SAME size. Only position or orientation changes!

➗ Fractions

  • Same denominator: add/subtract only the numerator
  • The denominator never changes
3/8 + 4/8 = 7/8  |  9/10 − 3/10 = 6/10
  • Numerical expressions: follow order of operations
  • Parentheses first, then × ÷, then + −
Example: (3 + 2) × 4 = 5 × 4 = 20
Example: 3 + 2 × 4 = 3 + 8 = 11
Read the expression carefully — parentheses change everything!

⏱️ Measurement

Time conversions
60 seconds= 1 minute
60 minutes= 1 hour
24 hours= 1 day
7 days= 1 week
12 months= 1 year
365 days= 1 year
Length units
1 km= 1,000 m
1 m= 100 cm
1 m= 1,000 mm
1 cm= 10 mm

📊 Data & Problem Solving

📊 Bar / Column graph
Compares quantities across categories.
Taller bar = bigger value.
Read: title → axes → find bar → read value.
📈 Line graph
Shows change over time.
Line going up = increasing.
Read: title → axes → find point → read value.
📋 Data table
Organizes data in rows and columns.
Find value: row ∩ column.
Always check what each row/column represents.
🔢 Solving word problems
1. Read the question carefully
2. Find the data in the graph/table
3. Choose the operation (+, −, ×, ÷)
4. Show your work and check the answer
Always read the title and axis labels FIRST — they tell you what the data is about!
